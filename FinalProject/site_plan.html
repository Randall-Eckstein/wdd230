Bountiful Foods Site Map

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide information about the fruits in and around California, as well as to provide a opportunity for users to create drinks while simultaneously tracking calories.

Target Audience

The average audience for this site are health-conscious individuals living in and around Los Angeles.

Site Map

This site will consist of three pages: the home page, fresh page, and about us page
