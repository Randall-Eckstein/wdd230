About Bountiful Foods

Bountiful Foods was created shortly after John Steinbeck wrote "East of Eden." The powerful image of the locomotives moving heads of lettuce up and down the California Coast spoke to Earl B. Freedman who saw the importance of fresh fruits and vegetables.

Following his acquisition of the fruit transportation rights along the west coast, Earl set up his first fruit drink shoppe.

As times have changed, Earl's family have kept up and are now offering juice smoothies with nutritional values. We don't add the accai berries, or ginseng powder of some other shops, but we do offer fresh fruit drinks. Fresh squeezed, fresh off the farm, and fresh for you.
