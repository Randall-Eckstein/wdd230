Target Audience

My target audience is expatriat or English-speaking users in Jilin City, China who would like to know more about what is going on in the city.

Personas

Persona: Casual user
Fictional name: Edward Smith
Job title/ major responsibilities: English teacher at a public high school
Demographics:
  • 32 years old
  • Unmarried
  • Has a bachelors in economics
  • Has a disposable income of $200 a month
Goals and tasks: Trying to find more information about fun or interesting activities or groups around the city.
Environment: He is extremely computer literate, and is constantly on social media. He uses the web for over 2 hours a day, and enjoys meeting new people online or via meet-and-greet activities found online.
Quote: "You never know who you're going to run into that will change your life."
Persona: Influencer
Fictional name: Zhang Mingwen
Job title/ major responsibilities: Chinese businesswoman; responsible for developing English activites in the community
Demographics:
  • 47 years old
  • Married
  • Has a Master's degree in Business English
  • Works for the city
  • Enjoys meeting new foreign friends, and takes pride in providing good opportunities for Chinese and foreign friends to meet and do business or make friendships.
Goals and tasks:

Encourage cross-culture communication, entrepreneurship, and long-lasting relationships with top-quality foreign talent.

Her salary and budget are tied to the government mandates for cultural exchange and expatriate engagement.
Environment: She is competent with the computer and the internet. She is frequently posting comments and notices for activities. She uses the web to publicize her events as well as network.
Quote: "Presentation and quality will ensure continued growth."
